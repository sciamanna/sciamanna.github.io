Change Delivery Manager

Regulatory Risk / Financial Crime - 2013 to 2019 – London

Captain the delivery of strategic, critical change management initiatives, leading high-impact, cross-functional set of on & offshore teams to ensure successful project & change execution.

Responsibilities included:

  • Deliver the business vision by leading change implementation on time, on budget and within risk appetite by fostering lasting, transparent & collaborative relationships with senior management, business SMEs, internal IT delivery partners & vendors to effect continual project success
  • Plan projects, estimate resource demands and chair progress meetings to provide Programme Delivery Board level tracking and reporting. Generate RAID logs and mandatory programme documentation
  • Facilitate discussion, decision making & conflict resolution & conduct data gathering & issue resolution workshops. Coach the team to higher levels of agile & scrum maturity, leading & collaborating with teams in India, the UK & the US
  • Prepare and clarify the project backlog and ensure that it gets managed appropriately while assisting and empowering the team in developing optimal solution strategies
  • Facilitate backlog prioritisation, estimation and planning. Facilitate daily stand-ups, sprint demos and retrospectives
  • Coordinate and manage user acceptance testing (UAT), including UAT script creation

Selected Contributions:

  • Successfully automated key Anti Bribery & Corruption (ABC) processes, acting as Scrum Master/Product Owner, leading a team of 8 developers & testers based in India; working alongside ABC SMEs based in London, Edinburgh and the US. The new systems improved the assessment of the ABC risk across the bank, closed the prioritised control gaps identified by regulators and enabled ABC to outsource most of the administrative time-consuming processes to cheaper locations. By extending my role from lead BA to Programme Manager the bank saved 25% of the project cost during 2018/2019
  • Drove a mandatory third-party software rollout programme across the US, UK, Ireland & the Channel Islands to enable Rules Mapping. I joined as the lead BA/Implementation Manager taking this responsibility from the vendor, I developed standardised process for each rollout, reduced the number or feeds and brought the report development inhouse saving the bank over £250K
  • Collaborated cross-functionally with IT and Compliance teams on the requirements for the Conflicts of Interest Programme. I ensured the data feeds (deal, company, employee, trade) to the new Compliance systems came from ‘golden sources’ leveraging existing licences & avoiding additional charges
  • Orchestrated and lead UAT for critical, multi-regional releases; cultivating positive vendor, consultant, and internal resource relationships; while coaching and mentoring a team of 5 consultants working in 4 Conflict of Interest workstreams with the systems being rolled out in 16 countries
  • Defined and established standard processes to support and execute legacy system decommissioning: I managed vendor, consultant and internal resource to identify and resolve data quality issues, mapping, cleaning data and coordinating data migrations. As a result, 8 systems were successfully decommissioned in 6 months, simplifying and standardising Compliance process & IT footprint
